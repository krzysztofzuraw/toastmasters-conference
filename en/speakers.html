Simon Bucknall

An award-winning, international speaker, Simon helps high-achieving professionals to achieve greater impact through the spoken word.

In 2017, Simon represented Europe in the Final of the World Championship of Public Speaking in Vancouver, Canada, placing 2nd out of 30,000+ competing speakers from all over the world.

Over the past decade, Simon has helped professionals from more than 100 countries and from all sectors - including FTSE 100 & Fortune 100 executives, entrepreneurs, politicians, MBAs and academics as well as leaders in the not-for-profit sector.

With experience coaching C-suite executives at the highest level, Simon’s clients include General Electric, HgCapital, Rede Partners, TrendMicro, DPD, London Business School, Oxford University’s Blavatnik School of Government, Cambridge University’s Laing O’Rourke Centre for Construction Engineering & Technology and the Royal Academy of Engineering.

Simon’s career background spans executive headhunting, blue-chip corporate brand strategy consulting plus a stint working in the House of Commons as Head of Office for a senior Parliamentarian. He has featured as an expert commentator on BBC World News, BBC London Radio, Channel 5 News and on international broadcast radio networks around the world.

Simon holds degrees from Oxford University and the London School of Oriental & African Studies. He is a Faculty member at Oxford University’s Blavatnik School of Government and a Visiting Fellow in Professional Skills at Cambridge University.

In 2013, he was awarded the Freedom of the City of London.

Keynote – Sex, Politics & Religion – 3 Keys For Speaking To Inspire

10:00 – 10:45 –– Saturday 14th of April

Married with two children, Simon Bucknall has worked in the British Parliament and was born and brought up a Catholic. So, when it comes to Sex, Politics & Religion, he’s speaking from first-hand experience.

Some people regard them as taboo, but these three themes also fire people’s emotions. When it comes to high-impact communication, what could be more important?

In this practical and entertaining keynote, 2017 World Championship of Public Speaking Finalist, Simon Bucknall offers key insights that will enable you to connect with, influence and inspire your audiences with significantly greater impact – as a leader, team member and speaker.

When it comes to winning hearts and minds, Sex, Politics & Religion have a vital role to play…

Workshop – Your Red-Hot Elevator Pitch – How to really engage a listener

13:00 – 13:45 –– Sunday 15th of April

You may be interviewing for a new role, wanting to make a good impression at a networking event or seeking buy-in from customers, colleagues or clients.

Many professionals struggle to sell either themselves or their ideas because they are pitched in the wrong way. When the pressure is on, how do you get people to really listen to you?

In this practical, interactive session, experience a simple, powerful process for structuring value in a clear and compelling way. Perfect for developing your skills as a networker and business developer, you’ll have the opportunity to hone your own short ‘pitch’ – whether it’s an idea, a product or yourself!

• Engage your listeners’ attention, right from the word go

• ‘Sell’ yourself and your ideas more effectively

• Be more memorable and build your personal brand
