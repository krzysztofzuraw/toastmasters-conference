Piotr Chimko

Manager with eighteen years of experience in IT, finance and procurement in large, multinational corporations. For more than three years, as business process manager, has contributed to global transformation of Philips. Passionate about effective communication and leadership.

Toastmaster since 2009, currently Program Quality Director responsible for education in District 95, which covers 18 countries in central, eastern and northern Europe.

Lukas Liebich

Career-wise: Project & Program Manager

Crazy about: Toastmasters. Since July 2017 he’s working as Club Growth Director for future District 110 (Wild East of Europe). Supporting Division Directors from Czechia to Croatia and Ukraine in achieving their Membership and Club growth targets.

Special skill: Reading books. Slowly.

Personal weakness: Sometimes takes himself too seriously. He’s fighting this terrible trait with support of his closest friends who don’t (take him too seriously). And by reading wise books.

What makes people nervous in his presence: He takes notes of brilliant / awkward things they say in his little black journal.

Adam Pioch

Founder of Toastmasters club in Poznan and Pila. He inspired dozens of people by public speaking, resulting in many successes and achievements of Toastmasters clubs in Poznan and development of clubs in other cities.

He specializes in one of the most demanding manuals in Toastmasters, i.e. Storytelling. It involves impersonating various characters, presenting dialogues, as well as intricate weaving of the dramaturgy of the story – what Adam additionally be speckles with a big dose of terror in a pure form. In everyday life a coach, trainer and mentor of many people engaged in writing and internet marketing. Teaches “public speaking” on MBA studies. A coach in the subject of websites auditing and storytelling.
